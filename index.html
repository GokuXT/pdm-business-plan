Market Strategy & Analysis

The Los Santos vehicle market is saturated with private sellers and generic lots. PDM differentiates itself through a curated inventory mix and specific targeting of emerging wealth classes in the city. We don't just sell Supers; we dominate the entry-level sport and luxury SUV markets.

Target Market Inventory Mix

Strategy: While Supercars draw attention, the volume sales come from Sports and SUVs. We maintain a 40% stock in high-turnover Sports vehicles to ensure steady cash flow while keeping 20% reserved for high-ticket Supercars.

PDM vs. Competitors (SWOT Metric)

The Edge: Competitors often focus solely on price. PDM leads in Customer Experience and Aftercare, offering free first-time repairs and customization consultations, justifying a premium price point.

Management Philosophy

Lead by Example

Management will be on the floor selling alongside staff. We do not sit in offices all day. This boosts morale and keeps leadership grounded in the reality of the market.

Conflict Resolution

Zero tolerance for toxicity. Internal disputes are handled via mediation. Customer disputes are handled with "The Customer is Key" mindset, but staff abuse is not tolerated.

Strict RP Adherence

All business operations, from invoices to test drives, must maintain high-quality immersion. No "OOC" (Out of Character) talk on the showroom floor.
